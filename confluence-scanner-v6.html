🌅 Pre-Market Preparation Mode
Use this before the open (4am–9:30am ET / 9am–2:30pm UK) to build your watchlist.
RVOL and FL Stage are unreliable pre-market — focus on gap%, news catalyst and short interest only.
At the open, move to the Live Scanner tab to find your entry.
1Run the scan — look for stocks gapping up 5%+ with a news reason. Write down your top 5.
2Check Finviz for each — note short float %. Above 15% = squeeze fuel. Type it into the SI% column.
3Check the news headline — do you understand WHY it's gapping? Earnings beat? FDA? M&A? No reason = avoid.
4Note the previous close — this is your reference level. Price should stay above it after the open.
5Pre-plan your levels — rough entry zone, stop below VWAP, T1 and T2 targets. Use the Trade Planner tool.
6At open — watch only your watchlist. Wait for the spike, the pullback, then the RESUME candle close. That's your entry.
